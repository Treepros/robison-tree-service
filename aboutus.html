About Us

At Robison Tree Service, we are dedicated to preserving and enhancing the natural beauty of your landscape. As a premier tree service provider in Lexington, Kentucky, we have been committed to delivering exceptional tree care services to both residential and commercial clients for over 25 years. Our commitment to community and safety sets us apart. We offer a comprehensive range of professional tree services, including tree pruning, tree removal, stump grinding, and emergency tree services, all delivered with expertise and care. Our certified arborists are equipped to handle all aspects of tree care, ensuring your property remains safe, healthy, and beautiful.

Our Mission

At Robison Tree Service, our mission is simple: to provide the highest level of professionalism and service to our customers. We prioritize safety and strive to build long-lasting relationships by consistently exceeding expectations. With complimentary evaluations and prompt responses to inquiries, we aim to find the best solutions to meet your tree care needs. Whether it’s routine maintenance or an emergency situation, we are here to ensure your trees stay in the best shape possible.
